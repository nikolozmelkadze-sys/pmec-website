Meet Your Trainer

Award-winning. Certified. Built for Georgia’s professionals.

Nikoloz Melkadze is a PMP®-certified project manager, educator, and consultant with over nine years of teaching experience at Georgian universities and a track record of delivering complex programs in high-stakes institutional environments. He holds active credentials from PMI and the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD), and holds a graduate degree from the University of Groningen.

His career includes senior leadership in the defense and security sector, most recently as Deputy Director at the Defence Institutional Building School, where he led capacity-building initiatives, organizational development programs, and strategic training efforts across institutional and international partners. In 2025, he was awarded the III Degree Medal for Immaculate Service by the Ministry of Defence of Georgia.

An active member of the PMI Tbilisi, Georgia Chapter, Nikoloz brings both rigorous PMI methodology and real-world delivery experience into every session. He trains and mentors professionals who want structured, honest preparation: no shortcuts, no filler.
